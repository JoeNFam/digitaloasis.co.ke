Our Story

Founded in 2018, Digital Oasis began with a mission: to make advanced networking and billing systems accessible to Kenyan businesses — from small cafes to large ISPs.


We specialize in MikroTik integration, helping clients automate hotspot billing, accept M-Pesa payments, and manage networks across multiple locations — all with enterprise-grade reliability.


Today, we serve over 150 clients across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and beyond. Our team of Cisco and MikroTik-certified engineers brings deep expertise in network design, cybersecurity, and user management systems.


We don’t just install routers — we build digital ecosystems that grow with your business.

Our Journey
